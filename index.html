About me

Why I'm excited about this project

I love designing for real spaces with real constraints.

I think in user flows

From my research work at SAHA Lab, I've learned to trace how data moves and how decisions get made. For your kiosk, that means designing the journey a visitor takes from first impression to "I want to book a consultation."

I move fast on pilots

I mentored 50+ students and organized hackathons—I'm comfortable with ambiguity, iterating on feedback, and shipping concepts that feel polished without needing months of refinement.

I obsess over interface clarity

Every pixel should serve a purpose. I design systems that scale, not one-off mockups. The EPA tool taught me that constraints spark creativity—limited screen space, high-traffic use, quick decision-making. This is exactly what a showroom kiosk is.
